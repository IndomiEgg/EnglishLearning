12 Tenses with Formulas and Examples

1. Simple Present

Formula: Subject + V1 (+s/es)

Example: She goes to school.

2. Present Continuous

Formula: Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

Example: I am eating lunch.

3. Present Perfect

Formula: Subject + has/have + V3

Example: They have finished the job.

4. Present Perfect Continuous

Formula: Subject + has/have been + V-ing

Example: He has been working since morning.

5. Simple Past

Formula: Subject + V2

Example: We visited the museum.

6. Past Continuous

Formula: Subject + was/were + V-ing

Example: She was reading a book.

7. Past Perfect

Formula: Subject + had + V3

Example: He had left before I came.

8. Past Perfect Continuous

Formula: Subject + had been + V-ing

Example: We had been waiting for hours.

9. Simple Future

Formula: Subject + will + V1

Example: I will call you.

10. Future Continuous

Formula: Subject + will be + V-ing

Example: They will be traveling tomorrow.

11. Future Perfect

Formula: Subject + will have + V3

Example: She will have graduated next year.

12. Future Perfect Continuous

Formula: Subject + will have been + V-ing

Example: He will have been working for 10 years.
